Primary Insurance Amount (PIA): The base monthly Social Security payout at FRA.
Full Retirement Age (FRA): This age is 67. Filing before FRA reduces monthly payment while filing after increases it.
Spousal benefit: A person may raise their Social Security payout up to half of their spouse's PIA. Filing before FRA reduces this benefit.
Survivor benefit: A person may raise their Social Security payout up to 100% of their deceased spouse's payout.
